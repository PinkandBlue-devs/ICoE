About Us

ICoE at a Glance

Indala College of Engineering is owned and managed by the Late. Bhaskarrao Mahajan Social, Educational and Cultural Society (LBMSECS). LBMSECS is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860.

It primarily focuses on the four major pillars of the students' development: Intellectual, Spiritual, Physical and Social. It also provides facilities for the pursuit of excellence and academically suitable climate of the scholarship in terms of curriculum and curricular performance of the students.

The goal of the proposed Institution, Scope and Justification of its establishment in the light of the prevailing Technical Education and Industrial scenario in the State. Our aim to provide valuable resources for industry and society through excellence in technical education and research and to provide necessary environment of multi-faceted opportunities to students to become a global player. The institute is located in serene campus amidst pristine nature.

Objectives of the Institution
1. To develop state-of-the-art infrastructure that promotes conductive ambience promoting innovation and research.
2. To develop functionality suitable and conductive envitonment for students and staff for academic purpose.
3. To impelement Effective and efficient Teaching-Learning practices.
4. To strengthen industry institute interaction.
5. Help students for personality development and career guidance.

About KBMSSSS Trust

KAI. BHASKARRAO MAHAJAN SAMAJIK SANSKRUTIK VA SHAISHNIK SANSTHA (KBMSSSS) Trust was formed in 2003, a Non-profit Making Organization, and trustees keep an honest aim to providing quality vocational and technical education to meet the challenges of the 21st Century.

This Trust is highly committed to the social and economic development of the society by adding the virtue of knowledge and creative practices among the youngsters
KBMSSSS Trust is committed to create encouraging environment and to provide the state of art facilities and freedom to the staff and students of Institute for efficient running of the Institute and for its commendable growth.

KBMSSSS Trust aspires to develop an Educational Complex which will be recognized all over the globe as one of the prominent and authentic Centers of Technological Training and Research. KBMSSSS Trust has proactive, capable and experienced team which aims to unlock the hidden potential in subconscious minds of students and to create competent Engineers, Pharmacist, Architect, and Skilled Manpower with said vision & social commitment.

INDALA GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS, Bapsai is a part of KAI. BHASKARRAO MAHAJAN SAMAJIK SANSKRUTIK VA SHAISHNIK SANSTHA (KBMSSSS) as an institution anticipates the momentum of globalization intensifying in the coming years which would make India even more attractive destination to learn and practice management. This is particularly felt, when mostly all Indian Industries are having major world class players from across the world.

Trust is providing educational services and is further proposing to diversify its services through setting up INDALA GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS comprising of Indala college of Engineering (ICoE), Indala Institute of Pharmacy (IIoP), Indala School of Architecture (ISoA), Indala Pvt Industrial Training Institution (iiTi) and Indala International School, Jr College (IIsJC) and Indala Skill Devotement Centre (IsDc). The Institute is spread over in an area of 13 acres of land and is located at 12.5 km from Kalyan Railway station (W), 6 km from Titwala Railway station and 13 km from Murbad. The area comes under Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA).

Well known educationalist, Mrs. Jayshree Mahajan, President, KBMSSSS, and all the trustee along with highly qualified staff, well developed Laboratories are focused to deliver the world class quality education to the students.
