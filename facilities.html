Computer Center

Indala College of Engineering (ICoE) is owned and managed by the Late. Bhaskarrao Mahajan Social, Educational and Cultural Society (LBMSECS). LBMSECS is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860. It primarily focuses on the four major pillars of the students' development: Intellectual, Spiritual, Physical and Social. It also provides facilities for the pursuit of excellence and academically suitable climate of the scholarship in terms of curriculum and curricular performance of the students. Library

The aim of the library is to provide the right environment and appropriate facilities and services to support high quality teaching and learning. The library provides a useful collection of learning materials, access to computerized information sources and audio/video learning facilities. The institute will have a library with 400 sq meter area which is sufficient for necessary facilities such as Photocopy Service, Multimedia Resource, Reference Service, Wi-Fi network connection and separate reading room etc. to be included with centrally air-conditioned system. The library is a subscriber of National and International Journals. It will be equipped with large number of Hand books / reference books in all disciplines.

It fulfills the entire requirements of the research scholars / faculty members and students in all disciplines of Engineering. The catalogue is available on web OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue) for the library’s entire document collection is available in bibliographical form. It has provided a much – needed relief to the library users in accessing, retrieving and using the learning resources of the library. The documents can be search by author/s & editors, any world/s from the title or subject heading/s. User friendly (and menu driven) help screens of the OPAC retrieval software help the user/s make search/es with ease and promptness.

Gymkhana

We encourage students to workout in gym for mental and physical fitness. The institute provides facility of state of the art gym. After all students who are motivated by fitness and wellness tend to have better management skills and research shows that being fit is good for the mind. It all ties together."

Cafeteria

A cafeteria with modern facilities is available within the campus and is open for staff, students and visitors. The Cafeteria with a seating capacity in excess of 100 serves healthy and hygienic food. The modern kitchen utilities available at the cafeteria help reduce the time taken to prepare each meal and also to serve it hot.

Facilities available for differently abled in the campus

Indala group ensures that each building block in the campus allows barrier free movement in order to provide safe and friendly environment for differently abled people. This includes provisions like elevators, ramps as alternative to steps in different building blocks, interconnection with different building blocks, special toilets ,separate parking lots, wheel chairs provisions and made other necessary changes to address the accessibility issues regarding to disability.

Medical Center

The Health Centre of Indala College of Engineering is well equipped. It has separate inpatient facility for Male and Female students. The health centre is manned by a healthcare assistant to provide medical care to all the students, staff and faculty in case of emergency at no cost. For us health of our student and staff comes first on our priority list.
