Courses Offered

ICoE is unique in imparting quality technical education in Mumbai University, offering programs in various fields of Engineering and Technology. All engineers work in group to design, fabricate, produce, test and supervise the manufacturing of complex products and systems i.e. the equipments and components for a number of large industries including Hospitals, IT industries, Electronic data processing systems for Communication and Defence, etc.

Computer Engineering

60 Seats

Computers are rapidly becoming a quintessential aspect which can be found everywhere. Here comes the importance of Computer Engineering. Computer Engineering involves developing computer and computer- based systems. Professionals who work with computer hardware and software are termed as Computer Engineers. The Department has developed many state-of-art air-conditioned laboratories with more than 100 desktop computers with high configuration in various fields of Computer Engineering

Mechanical Engineering

60 Seats

Mechanical Engineering envisages the development, design, manufacturing and maintenance of machinery. The present age demands Mechanical Engineering specialists who have the capacity of adaptability and creativity in the new technical areas. Mechanical Engineers should have knowledge not only in their own specialized fields but also in wide interdisciplinary fields as well. To meet the above requirements Mechanical Engineering department offers the UG course in Mechanical Engineering.

Electrical Engineering

60 Seats

Electrical Engineering is one of the most sophisticated branches of engineering. Electrical Engineering plays has a major role in improving productivity in industries like oil, energy, agriculture and so many other important sectors of economy and improve the socio-economy status of our country. Electrical Engineering is a rapidly growing sector where there is excellent job opportunity for the skilled professionals to lead a challenging and lucrative career.

Civil Engineering

60 Seats

Civil engineering is one of the oldest of the engineering professions. Civil engineers are responsible for building good quality infrastructure/systems. A civil engineer is concerned with determining the right design for these structures and looking after the construction process so that the longevity of these structures is guaranteed after completion. These structures should also be satisfactory for the public in terms of comfort.
